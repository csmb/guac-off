Third Annual

Indian Summer Guac-Off

About
The Annual Indian Summer Guac-off is a party that showcases the best guacamole's that the City has to offer. The fine humans of San Francisco will get to decide who has the best guacamole in the City. The winner to be crowned with the guacamole glory trophy, bragging rights until next Indian summer, the title of official "Guacamole of San Francisco", and a secret mystery prize!
Rules
Entry into the competition is free. Your guacamole must use at least 6 avocados. For all others wishing to attend, you must fairly judge the guacamole's presented.
Prizes
Stellar prizes from some stellar local companies!
Location
Saturday, Sept 13th, 2013
230 Guerrero St, SF
2pm - until the last chip is eaten
