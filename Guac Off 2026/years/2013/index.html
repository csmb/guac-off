About

Mission Guac Party is a party that will be showcasing the best Guacamoles that the city has to offer.

The party going people of San Francisco will get to decide who has the best guacamole in the City!

The winner to be crowned with the guacamole glory trophy, bragging rights until next Indian Summer, the title of official "Guacamole of San Francisco", and a secret mystery prize!

Rules

Entry into the competition is free

Your guacamole must use at least 5 avocados

For all others wishing to attend, you must fairly judge the Guacamoles presented

What we're giving away:

Stellar secret prizes from some stellar local companies!

Our buddies at Dear Mom will be there!

...and of course the #PandaTweet will be there to enjoy guacamole with all!
